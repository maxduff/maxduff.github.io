Mark Aldanov

Russian writer

Here is a timeline of Marl Aldanov's life:

  • 1886 - Born in Kiev in the family of a rich Jewish sugar industrialist
  • 1910 - Graduates from Kiev univeristy in law and natural science, with a specialty in chemistry.
  • 1915 - Writes an essay called Tolstoï and Romain Rolland.
  • 1919 - Exiles himself to France.
  • 1922 - Lives in Berlin, before going back to France in 1924.
  • 1924 - Marries his cousin, Tatiana Markovna Zaïtseff.
  • etc etc...
